Lore

Blockman is back, and better than ever. In the wake of defeating the 30 tyrants, Blockman failed to foresee the age-old result of a coup - removing the leadership creates a power vacuum. Before the new warlords fight each other for power, they all agreed that Blockman was a threat and have banded together to elimate him. Can Blockman survive thier onslaught? Survive 25 levels of fast-paced projectile dodging while making use of 6 skills and perks in BLOCKMAN 2: Reckoning.

About the Devs

This game was designed and created by Shemar Gordon and Jarrit Alicea, two fellows of the 2020 Marcy Lab School Cohort. This was created for an open-ended project to make a game using what we have learned about the DOM, JavaScript and beyond. We hope you enjoy it! The music is Shemar's original sequencing of the Jamaican "Applause Riddim" originally produced by Cordell "Skatta" Burrell as a part of the Greensleves Album.

Every level you gain grants you a skill point. Every 5 levels, gain an additional skill point. Each skill maxes out at level 5, and each level of each skill has a minimum level requirement before you can spend a skill point on it. If a skill is maxed, then the perk for that skill is unlocked.
Max Health Each level adds +1 to maximum health
Healthy Health pick ups now heal +2 health
Armor Each level adds an 8% chance to block all damage
Aegis If damage is taken, damage is reduced by 1
Speed Each level increases speed by 0.6
C-Speed Speed pick ups now grant invincibility
Luck Each level adds increases power ups spawn chance by 20%
Fortunate Causes 2 powerups to spawn at a time
Adrenaline Adrenaline stops time for all bullets. Each level adds +1s to adrenaline time
Reflexes Getting hit reduces adrenaline cool down by 5s
Size Each level reduces your size by 10%
Relativity Reduces the size of all enemies by 30%
Kaboom Clears the area of bullets
Health Heals +1 HP. Grants +10 exp if health is full
Experience Grants +7.5% of experience required to level up
